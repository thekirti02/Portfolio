About Me

FrontEnd Developer

Hi, I'm Kirti, a final-year Computer Science student at IGDTUW with a strong foundation in web development. Over the years, I've honed my skills in front-end development and expanded my expertise to full-stack development, particularly with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js).

My journey as a developer started with simple web pages, but my curiosity and passion led me to create more complex projects, from interactive games like Flappy Bird and Candy Crush to full-fledged web applications. I thrive on solving problems and bringing ideas to life through code.

In addition to my technical skills, I am constantly learning and staying updated with the latest trends in web development. My goal is to build scalable, user-friendly applications that make a difference. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, working on side projects, or enjoying time with friends and family.
